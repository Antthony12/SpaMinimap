🏁 Spa-Francorchamps Minimap for GTA V — full lap around the circuit

In this video I take a full lap of Spa-Francorchamps in GTA V using my Spa-Francorchamps Minimap mod, a script that displays a live map on screen with your position over the real circuit — just like the classic Nürburgring mod, but adapted to Spa using real telemetry recorded on the actual track.

🔧 About the mod
- Map with the real Spa-Francorchamps layout, generated from thousands of telemetry points recorded while completing a lap of the circuit.
- Live pin that follows your position on the map in real time.
- The map appears automatically only when you are on the circuit and disappears when you leave it.
- Map position and size fully configurable from an .ini file.
- Open source (MIT).
